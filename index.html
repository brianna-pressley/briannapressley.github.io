The Evil Calculator

Welcome to The Evil Calculator — where math meets mischief! Inspired by the bright neon lights and checkerboard floors of retro diners, this project gives classic math tools a playful 1950s twist. Grab a milkshake, tap a few buttons, and let chaos (and charm) do the rest.

About the Creator

Hi, I am Brianna Pressley — a web design student with a love for all things vintage and vibrant. I wanted to make a calculator that feels like it came straight out of a 1950s diner — a little loud, a little flashy, and full of personality. My goal is to mix fun design with playful interactivity.

“Don't take math too seriously — even calculators need a break.”
